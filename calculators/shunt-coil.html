Antenna Matching Tool

Shunt Matching Coil Calculator

Most mobile HF antennas resonate at 20–30Ω rather than the 50Ω your transceiver expects. A shunt coil placed in parallel at the antenna feedpoint transforms that lower impedance up to 50Ω without lossy resistive components. Enter your measured antenna resistance and operating frequency to find the required inductance and winding details.
