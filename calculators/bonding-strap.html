RF Grounding Tool

Bonding Strap Impedance Calculator

Flat copper braid exploits the skin effect to minimize RF impedance — its wide, thin geometry maximizes the surface area where HF current actually flows. Round wire of the same DC resistance is dramatically worse at HF frequencies, because inductive reactance dominates and the skin depth leaves most of the conductor unused.
