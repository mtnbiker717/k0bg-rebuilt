Mobile Antenna Efficiency Estimator

Updated April 20, 2026

Every watt your transmitter puts out gets divided three ways before it leaves the vehicle: radiation, coil loss, and ground loss. This calculator makes that split visible. Enter your antenna parameters and see exactly where your 100 watts go.
