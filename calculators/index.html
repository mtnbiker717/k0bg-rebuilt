Interactive Calculators

Browser-based tools — no server required

These tools put K0BG's installation guidelines into interactive form. Enter your specific components and conditions — results update in real time. All calculations run entirely in your browser.

All Tools

⚙️

Ferrite Choke Designer

Find the right mix, bead size, and turns for any HF choke. Plots impedance from 1–30 MHz across all ham bands.
🔌

Wire & Fuse Sizing

Calculate minimum wire gauge, correct fuse rating, and voltage drop for any transceiver or amplifier installation.
🌀

Shunt Matching Coil

Given your antenna's input impedance, calculate the inductance and winding dimensions for a proper shunt matching coil.
📏

Bonding Strap Impedance

See why flat braid beats round wire for RF. Visualizes skin effect and computes impedance vs. frequency for any strap.
📡

Antenna Efficiency

The ARRL "3 Rs" made interactive. See exactly how much of your 100W is actually being radiated vs. lost to coil and ground.
🗺️

Vehicle Bonding Map

Click-to-bond your vehicle. Track which ground points are complete and print a checklist to take to the garage.

Based on the original work of Alan Applegate, K0BG. Preserved for the amateur radio community.

Important Notice: Calculator results are engineering approximations based on published component data. Verify with a NanoVNA or other test equipment for critical installations. This site is not affiliated with or endorsed by Alan Applegate (K0BG).
