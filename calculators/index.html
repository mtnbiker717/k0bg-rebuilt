Interactive Calculators

Browser-based tools — no server required

These tools put the math behind K0BG's installation guidelines into interactive form. Plug in your components and conditions — results update in real time. All calculations run in your browser; nothing is sent to a server.

Ferrite Choke Designer

Select mix, bead size, and turns count — plots impedance from 1–30 MHz with band markers and a resistive vs. reactive breakdown.

Open calculator →

Wire & Fuse Sizing

Enter peak current draw and run length — returns minimum AWG, correct fuse rating, and voltage drop with a live wiring diagram.

Open calculator →

Shunt Matching Coil

Enter your antenna's measured input impedance — calculates the inductance and winding dimensions for a proper shunt coil.

Open calculator →

Bonding Strap Impedance

Compare flat braid vs. round wire at any frequency. Visualizes skin effect and computes RF impedance for any strap geometry.

Open calculator →

Antenna Efficiency Estimator

The ARRL three-Rs model made interactive. Shows exactly how much of your 100W is radiated vs. lost to coil resistance and ground.

Open calculator →

Vehicle Bonding Map

Click to bond your vehicle's ground points. Tracks score, prioritizes next steps, and prints a shop checklist.

Open calculator →
📐 About the math

These calculators use established engineering approximations — the same formulas behind Fair-Rite's published data, the ARRL Antenna Book, and standard NEC tables. Results are accurate enough for installation planning. For critical installations, verify with a NanoVNA or calibrated test equipment.

Based on the original work of Alan Applegate, K0BG. Preserved for the amateur radio community.
